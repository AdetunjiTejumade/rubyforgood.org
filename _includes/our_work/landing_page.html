Work With Nonprofits

We have earned the trust of large and small nonprofits by understanding their needs and working with them to deliver high quality software and technical solutions to lighten their workloads and improve their processes.

We build meaningful relationships with our nonprofit partners, meet with them to truly understand their problems, and develop solutions customized to their problem so they don't have to try and use something generic and try and make it work.

Hold Events

We love to get together, build software, learn from each other and have fun.

We have large gatherings with a hundred or more attendees and small focused ones with a dozen or so people.

We facilitate company offsites where RfG folks work with members of their team together on building impactful software for our nonprofit partners.

Corporate Volunteering

We're delighted to work with companies to give their employees a meaningful way to volunteer.

We can do everything -- find the venue, organize lodging, meals and food, arrange nonprofit stakeholders, github issues, everything for your peeps to show up and work.

We can work with you, we'll supply RfG folks to onboard your people during your volunteering event, answer questions and ensure you have a successful event.

We can set up projects and take a hands off approach and just let your people contribute.
