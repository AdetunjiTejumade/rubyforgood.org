Our Nonprofit Partners

We have earned the trust of diverse nonprofits, located nationwide, by learning about their needs and collaborating with them to deliver high quality software and technical solutions to build business efficiencies and streamline their processes.

We build meaningful, long-lasting relationships with our nonprofit partners, immerse ourselves in their business processes to gain an in-depth understanding of their challenges. This enables us to develop solutions that are customized to their needs to ensure that they have a solution that is designed for their specific industry and mission, which drives much more impact than out-of-the-box, generic solutions that are often on the market as the only viable option.

Corporate Volunteering

We are delighted to work with organizations to provide their employees with meaningfulvolunteering opportunitiesHere is more information on our volunteer packages:

1) We facilitate all aspects of your skills based volunteering event. We'll find an amazing venue for your folks to come to, we'll arrange the lodging, the food, fun team building exercises and games for when you're not write code, bring nonprofit stakeholders on site for your people to interact with and see who they are helping, have curated issues for your developers to work on, and everything else all thought out so your folks have a fun, impactful, and meaningful experience.

2) We come to you and make your volunteering as easy as possible. We will bring Ruby for Good developers, product leads, and nonprofit stakeholders on site to work with your team to answer technical and nontechnical questions ensuring they have a great experience. We will also curate issues for developers to work on so they are able to have a productive event.

3) We will curate issues, make nonprofit stakeholders available, and stand back and let your folks take the lead on all development.
